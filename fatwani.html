Proposals [Read critique]

1. Technology Programming Society

Overview and Scope : I propose to establish a Technology Programming Society for the benefit of the numerous students interested in learning coding across all the years. Programming and its related facets have become the need of the hour. The number of opportunities available to the students with a good knowledge of programming is growing at a steeper rate than ever. This society will help foster the interest of students in coding and will provide them numerous opportunities to learn and improve their programming skills in various areas.

Activities :

  • Social Cell shall be organizing various social events in the campus and this shall be done in association with various NGOs which will be contacted regularly.
  • The Social Cell will also provide the various NGOs a platform to display to the students and faculty at IIT Kharagpur the work which they are doing for the society.
  • The Social Cell shall also, as per the requirements of concerned NGOs, arrange for volunteers to work for the NGOs as social internships. ALL the expenses will be borne by the NGO.

2. Information Portal for GC

Overview and Scope : I propose to introduce an information dissemination portal regarding the events held under the banner of Technology General Championships so that all the students at IIT Kharagpur especially the post graduate students and research scholars of this institute. This portal will be the first source of information regarding the required know-how of the various events, timeline and schedule of the events along with proper discussion forums for doubt clearance related to the events of Technology General Championship.

3. Webinar Series for Kshitij

Overview : I propose to introduce a webinar series in Kshitij so that those personalities who could not be physically present during Kshitij at IIT Kharagpur could still share their views and opinions and also motivate the youth of IIT Kharagpur with their experience. The Webinar series will also generate some interest amongst the students of IIT Kharagpur towards Kshitij which has been on a decline for the past few years.
