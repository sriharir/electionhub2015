1. International Recognition for Kshitij

  • a. Association with HPAIR i.e., Harvard Project for Asian and International Relations :The Harvard Project for Asian and International Relations (HPAIR)​is a student­run organization of the Harvard University Faculty of Arts and Sciences. HPAIR organizes Harvard University’s largest annual student conferences in the Asia­Pacific region. Since 1991, they have continuously gathered a growing pool of international students from top universities, renowned academics, business professionals, and political leaders to engage in their rigorous educational events. Their programs intend to achieve three goals: 1) Provide a dynamic forum of exchange on international issues vital to Asia 2) Foster long­term relationships among young leaders from all around the globe 3)Connect talented delegates with some of today’s leaders in academia, business and government.

  • b. To obtain International Organisation for Standardization(ISO) certification for Kshitij : International Organization for Standardization is a non­profit, non­government organization, which develop standards for products and services. These standards are accepted worldwide as the method to ensure that products and services are safe, reliable and of good quality. It is a network of national standards bodies that make up the ISO membership. Benefits to Kshitij : 1)Well defined and documented procedures improve the consistency of the output. 2) Quality is constantly measured. 3) Procedures ensure corrective action is taken whenever defects occur.

2. Online Financial Market Trading Portal

Overview and Scope : ​To introduce a ​ financial market online training portal ­ Elearnmarkets.com. Elearn Markets is an online education platform, which is committed to take finance training to the next level. Elearn markets is the ideal web portal for all those students who want to expand their horizon of knowledge and enhance their skill­set with respect to finance and capital markets.

Features :

  • This portal remains open for 24x7 and is unique in its approach to any where and any time education.
  • It provide the students with a blend of guidance material in different formats like important workbooks, course material, video lectures on important topics etc.
  • Students are being given the scope to study at their own pace and because the course material is available online, the students can access lectures, watch the videos again and again at any time they want and from any place, thus helping them to learn at their own pace.
  • Elearnmarkets.com portal also gives you the detailed analysis of the tests that you have taken and helps you to pinpoint your problematic area and then provides you with means to overcome those problems in our Forum (ask.elearnmarkets.com) where experts from various fields answer students' queries. It is updated regularly.
  • The knowledge analysis of Kredent Academy, have created mock tests that students can attempt to solve within the bounds of timing to overcome their apprehensions about real life tests and examinations.

3. Technology GC Web Portal

Overview and Scope : There does not exist a centralized system mechanism for students to find out about ongoing events in the Technology General Championship, the current standings and the basic information required to participate in the events of the Technology General Championship. Students interested to participate in various events often do not get the proper guidance and materials required.This is also one of the reasons for the lower turnout of the students for Technology General Championship.It will contain the basic information about the event, current standings of different Halls of Residences and pre­requisite required to participate in the event.

Features :

  • The website would contain previous years’ Problem Statements so that students would get an idea so as to in which way they should proceed.
  • The website would contain all the information about all the four cups namely, Knowledge Cup, Application Cup, Innovation cup and Strategy cup.
